Steph Vass

It is a testament to his will that Stephano was raised in the backwaters of Springs, yet has turned out to be a relatively normal person. Since his ears could work they have been turning sounds into music, he hears music in even the chaos. This allows him to appreciate genres typically unclassfiable to almost all other people: such as Crap-plopping; hadeda-and-baby-cries ; and most notably the bark of the Duchess of Linksfield.
