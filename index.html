Steph Vass

Born in Joburg, based in Cape Town, his quasi-formless and fluid brand of progressive music has lead him to easily flow across genres, leaving style unbound.

This versatility makes directing the energy on almost any dancefloor seemless. Through the melodic realm he has found a way to carve out light in even the darkest of sounds. Steph Vass has patiently been cutting his teeth in the industry at festivals and clubs across the country, namely:

  • A nice club
  • Funky club #76
  • Festival Blabla
  • Me mum's basement
  • Da KFC Boggs
